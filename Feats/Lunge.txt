:: Lunge ::
[Fighter]

Prequisites:
	Combat expertise
Benefit:
	When taking the full attack action, one of your attacks may be made as though its reach were 5 ft. greater, including with a reach weapon. You may still attack an opponent with the normal reach of your weapon with this attack.

Normal:
	A medium character can only exceed their standard 5 ft. reach by utilizing a reach weapon.

Special:
	A fighter may select lunge as one of their fighter bonus feats.
