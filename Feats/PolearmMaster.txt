:: Polearm Master ::
[Fighter]

Prequisites:
	Base attack bonus +3, proficiency with a reach weapon
Benefit:
	When using a reach weapon, you may attack enemies adjacent (within 5 ft. for a medium creature) to you in addition to enemies at reach (5-10 ft. away for a medium creature).

Normal:
	A medium creature can only use a reach weapon to attack enemies 10 ft. away and not adjacent enemies.

Special:
	A fighter may select polearm master as one of their fighter bonus feats.
