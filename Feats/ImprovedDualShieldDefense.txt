:: Improved Dual Shield Defense ::
[Fighter]

Prequisites:
	Improved shield bash, dual shield defense, fighter level 6th

Benefit:
	When wielding a shield in both hands, you may add the entire shield bonus of your off-hand shield to your AC, instead of one-half.

Normal:
	A character fighting with two shields using dual shield defense receives only one-half of its shield bonus to their AC.

Special:
	A fighter may select improved dual shield defense as one of his fighter bonus feats.
