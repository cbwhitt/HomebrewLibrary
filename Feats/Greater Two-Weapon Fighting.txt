:: Improved Two-Weapon Fighting ::
[Fighter]

Prerequisites:
	Dex 17, Two-Weapon Fighting

Benefit:
	The character can make as many attacks with their off-hand weapon as with their primary weapon, using the same base attack bonus. The character still takes the normal penalties for fighting with two weapons.

Normal:
	Without this feat, you can only get a single attack with an off-hand weapon.

Special:
	A fighter may select Improved Two-Weapon Fighting as one of their fighter bonus feats.
