:: Finesse Form ::
[Fighter]

Choose one type of weapon that does solely piercing or slasing damage. You may now benefit from the Weapon Finesse feat when wielding this weapon.

Prerequisites:
	Proficiency with selected weapon, Weapon Finesse, fighter level 2nd

Benefit:
	When wielding the selected weapon, attacks you make with it benefit from the Weapon Finesse feat.

Normal:
	Only light weapns, rapiers, whips, spiked chains, or other specially designated weapons can benefit from Weapon Finesse.

Special:
	A fighter may select Finesse Form as one of their fighter bonus feats.
