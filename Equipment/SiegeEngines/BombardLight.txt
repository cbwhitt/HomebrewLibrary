:: Bombard, Light ::
Direct ranged siege weapon

Cost: 2,000 gp
Ammunition Cost: 3 gp
Damage: 3d10 bludgeoning
Critical: x3
Base Accuracy: +0
Range Increment: 150 ft.
Maximum Crew: 4
Mount: Light
Aim Actions: 1
Reload Actions: 4

The light bombard--also known as the perier or stone gun--is a relatively short and light piece designed to fire round stone shot with small charges of powder.
The light bombard takes up a space 5 feet across and weighs 500 pounds.

Four actions to reload the bombard:
One full-round action and a DC 10 Profession (siege engineer) check to swab out bore;
one full-round action and DC 15 Profession (siege engineer) check to load powder and wad;
one full-round action and DC 10 Strength check to load stone shot;
one full-round action and DC 10 Strength check to tamp the shot.

One full-round action to aim the bombard.
