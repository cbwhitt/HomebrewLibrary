:: Frostbomb ::
Cost: 50 gp

A frostbomb is like a firebomb, except filled with alchemist's frost instead of alchemists' fire.
When the frostbomb hits its target, it bursts open and deals 3d8 points of frost damage in a 10-foot radius, DC 15 Reflex save for half.
No damage is dealt on the following round.
