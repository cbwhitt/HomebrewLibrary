:: Clubkins (5) ::
Exotic Light Thrown Ammunition
Critical: x3
Ranged Increment: 10 ft.
Type: Bludgeoning
Hardness: 10
Damage: 1d4
Weight: 1 lb.
HP: 1
Cost: 3 gp

	A clubkin can't be used as a melee weapon.
	Although they are thrown weapons, clubkins are treated as ammunition for the purposes of drawing them and crafting masterwork or otherwise special versions of them. A clubkin that hits its target is not destroyed. One that misses has a 20% chance of being lost.
	Prices and weights are for five clubkins.
	A clubkin is a special mystic weapon. This designation gives a mystic wielding clubkins special options.
