:: Sand Blades ::
Exotic One-Handed Thrown Melee
Critical: x3
Range Increment: 20 ft.
Type: Slashing
Hardness: 10
Damage: 1d6
Weight: 3 lb.
HP: 5
Cost: 25 gp

	You can use this weapon to make a ranged trip attack against an opponent. You can't be tripped during your own trip attempt when using a set of sand blades.
	You can use this weapon to make a ranged disarm attack against an opponent. You can't be disarmed during your own disarm attempt when using a set of sand blades.
	Qeria treat sand blades as martial weapons.
