:: Guisette ::
Martial One-Handed Melee
Critical: x3
Type: Slashing
Damage: 1d6
Weight: 9 lb.
Cost: 14 gp

	A guisette has reach. You can strike opponents 10 ft. away with it, but you can't use it against an adjacent foe. You can use a guisette to make trip attacks. If you are tripped during your own trip attempt, you can drop the guisette to avoid being tripped.
