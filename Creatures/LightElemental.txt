:: Light Elemental ::

Light elementals of all sizes share the following traits:
-----
Type: Elemental (Light, Extraplanar)
Special Attacks: Light mastery, ray
Special Qualities: Shield of light, elemental traits
Environment: Elemental Plane of Light
Organization: Solitary, pair, or essence (2-3 plus 3-6 smaller elementals)
Treasure: None
Alignment: Usually lawful good
Advancement: 1 HD (tiny), 2-3 HD (small), 4-7 HD (medium), 8-15 HD (large), 16-20 HD (huge), 21-23 HD (greater), 24+ HD (elder)
Level Adjustment: --

Light Mastery (Ex):
	A light elemental receives a +1 bonus on attack and damage rolls against creatures in bright light.

	Light elementals receive Improved Initiative as a bonus feat.
