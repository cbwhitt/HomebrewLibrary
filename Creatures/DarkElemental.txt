:: Dark Elemental ::

Dark elementals of all sizes share the following traits:
-----
Type: Elemental (Dark, Extraplanar)
Speed: 30 ft. (6 squares)
Special Attacks: Dark mastery, draining grasp
Special Qualities: See in darkness, elemental traits
Environment: Elemental Plane of Dark
Organization: Solitary, pair, or essence (2-3 plus 3-6 smaller elementals)
Treasure: None
Alignment: Usually neutral evil
Advancement: 1 HD (tiny), 2-3 HD (small), 4-7 HD (medium), 8-15 HD (large), 16-20 HD (huge), 21-23 HD (greater), 24+ HD (elder)
Level Adjustment: --

Dark Mastery (Ex):
	A dark elemental receives a +1 bonus on attack and damage rolls against creatures in darkness.

See in Darkness (Ex):
	A dark elemental can see perfectly in normal and magical darkness, even that created by a darkness spell.

Draining Grasp (Su):
	Against living creatures, a dark elementalâ€™s slam attack deals additional damage. The elemental gains a number of temporary hit points equal to the damage dealt this way. The elemental can't gain more temporary hit points than the subject's current hit points. The temporary hit points disappear 1 hour later.
