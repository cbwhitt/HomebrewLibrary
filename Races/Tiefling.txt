:: Tiefling ::

	See Planetouched MM entry.

	Outsider (native)
	+2 Dexterity, +2 Intelligence, -2 Constitution, -2 Charisma
	Tiefling base land speed is 30 ft.
	Medium: As Medium creatures, tieflings receive no special bonuses or penalties due to their size.
	Darkvision
	+2 racial bonus on Bluff and Hide checks
	Spell-Like Abilities: 1/day - 'darkness'. Caster leve 3rd, save DC 10 + tiefling's Int modifier + spell level.
	Automatic Languages: Common and Infernal
	Bonus Languages: Draconic, Dwarf, Elven, Gnome, Goblin, Halfling, Orc, Noctan
	Favored Class: Rogue
