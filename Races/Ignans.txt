:: Ignans ::

	+2 Charisma, -2 Wisdom
	Outsider (native, fire, primordial)
	Medium: As Medium creatures, ignans have no special bonuses or penalties due to their size.
	Ignan base land speed is 30 feet.

	+2 racial bonus on Diplomacy and Gather Information checks

	Automatic Languages: Common and Ignan
	Bonus Languages: Any
	Favored Class: Elementalist
	Level Adjustment: +0
