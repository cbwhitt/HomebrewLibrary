:: Aurans ::

	+2 Dexterity, -2 Strength 
	Outsider (native, air, primordial)
	Medium: As Medium creatures, aurans have no special bonuses or penalties due to their size.
	Auran base land speed is 40 feet.
	Darkvision
	Light as Air: Aurans are preternaturally light weight (about a tenth of a human of equivalent size). They receive a +12 racial bonus on Jump checks and +8 racial bonus on Climb checks (this includes the bonus associated with having a land speed greater than 30 feet). An auran has no fly speed, unlike most creatures of the air subtype.
	+1 racial bonus on attack rolls against terrans
	+2 racial bonus on Spot checks
	+2 racial bonus on Tumble checks
	Automatic Languages: Common and Auran
	Bonus Languages: Aven, Gnome, Gnoll, Goblin, Orc, Draconic.
	Favored Class: Elementalist
	Level Adjustment: +0
