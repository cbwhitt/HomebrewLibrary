:: Aasimar ::

	See Planetouched MM entry.

	Outsider (native)
	+2 Wisdom, +2 Charisma, -2 Intelligence, -2 Dexterity
	An aasimar's starting Intelligence score is always at least 3. If this adjustment would lower the character's score to 1 or 2, her score is nevertheless 3. 
	Aasimar base land speed is 30 ft.
	Medium: As Medium creatures, aasimar have no special bonuses or penalties do to their size.
	Darkvision
	+2 racial bonus on Spot and Listen checks
	+2 racial bonus on Sense Motive checks
	Spell-Like Abilities: 1/day - 'light'. Caster level 1st, save DC 10 + aasimar's Wis modifier + spell level.
	Automatic Languages: Common and Celestial
	Bonus Languages: Draconic, Dwarf, Elven, Gnome, Halfling, Sylvan, Luman
	Favored Class: Paladin
