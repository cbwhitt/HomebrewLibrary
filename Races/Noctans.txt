:: Noctans ::

	+2 Constitution, -2 Intelligence
	A noctan's starting Intelligence score is always at least 3. If this adjustment would lower the character's score to 1 or 2, her score is nevertheless 3.
	Outsider (native, dark)
	Medium: As Medium creatures, noctans have no special bonuses or penalties due to their size.
	Noctan base land speed is 30 feet.
	Darkvision 120 feet
	Humanoid Anatomy: Unlike other elementals, noctans have discernible anatomies and are therefore subject to critical hits and flanking. Additionally, noctans are not immune to poison, 'sleep' effects, paralysis, or stunning like other elementals are, and they must eat, sleep, and breathe.
	Fearmonger: Noctans are immune to magical fear effects, and the DC of [Fear] spells cast by Noctans increases by 1.
	+1 racial bonus on attack rolls against lumans
	+2 racial bonus on Intimidate checks
	+4 racial bonus on Hide checks
	Automatic Languages: Common and Noctan.
	Bonus Languages: Abyssal, Infernal, Undercommon, Elf, Orc, Gnoll, Goblin, 
	Spell-Like Abilities: 1/day - 'cause fear'. Caster level 1st; save DC 10 + noctan's Wisdom modifier + spell level.
	Favored Class: Elementalist
	Level Adjustment: +0
