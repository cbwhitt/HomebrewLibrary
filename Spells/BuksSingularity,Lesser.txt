:: Buk's Singularity, Lesser ::
Level: Sor/Wiz 2, Kenomancer 2
Components: V,S
Casting Time: 1 standard action
Range: Close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
Effect: A single point of force
Duration: 1 round / level (D)
Saving Throw: Fortitude negates
Spell Resistance: Yes

This spell creature a single point of force that draws objects and creatures within 5 ft. + 5 ft./3 levels of the point to itself. The first time any creature enters...

A Tiny or smaller creature or object is drawn to the point, rising into the air if the point is above the ground. A Tiny or smaller creature will take 1d4 nonlethal damage if it ends its turn within 5' of the point due to the crushing force.

Small creatures and objects are drawn to the point, but will not rise into the air if the point is above the ground. Flying Small creatures will be drawn into the point regardless of whether it is above the ground or above the ground or on the ground. A Small creature will take 1d3 nonlethal damage if it ends its turns within 5' of the point due to the crushing force.

Medium creatures and objects are not drawn to the point, but are unable to move away. A Medium creature will take 1d2 nonlethal damage if it ends its turn within 5' of the point due to the crushing force.

Large or larger creatures and objects are unaffected by this spell.

Any creature, regardless of size, takes a -4 penalty on ranged attacks that pass through the area of this spell, except for ranged attacks aimed at the singularity.

Buk's Singularity, Lesser, can be made permanent with a permanency spell.
