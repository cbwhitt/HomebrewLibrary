:: Energy Alteration ::
Transmutation
Level: Armorer 1
Components: V, S, F
Target: Magic item
Duration: 10 min./level
Saving Throw: Will negates (object)
Spell Resistance: Yes (object)

You alter an item that employs an energy type or grants protection from an energy type to employ or protect against a different energy type. For example, you could change a +2 flaming battleaxe so that it deals sonic damage. The amount of damage dealt, protection offered, and all other effects remain the same.
