:: Stone Construct ::
Transmutation
Level: Armorer 3
Components: S, F
Target: Construct
Duration: 10 min./level or until discharged
Saving Throw: Will negates (harmless)
Spell Resistance: Yes (harmless)

The construct imbued with this infusion gains resistance to blows, cuts, stabs, and slashes. It gain damage reduction 10/adamantine. Once the spell has prevented a total of 10 points of damage per level (maximum 150 points), it is discharged.
