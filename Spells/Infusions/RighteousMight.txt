:: Righteous Might ::
Transmutation [Varies, see text]
Level: Armorer 5
Components: V, S, F
Target: Item or construct

As the spell of the same name. You may select when imbuing the infusion whether this infusion grants damage reduction overcome by evil or good, and this infusion is a good or evil infusion, respectively. A creature of opposite alignment to the descriptor of the infusion cannot benefit from its effects.
