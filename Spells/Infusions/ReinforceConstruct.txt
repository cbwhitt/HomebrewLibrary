:: Reinforce Construct ::
Transmutation
Level: Armorer 2
Components: V, S, F
Target: Construct
Duration: 1 hour/level or until discharged; see text
Saving Throw: Will negates (harmless)
Spell Resistance: No

You bolster a construct's frame making it better able to withstand blows. The construct gains temporary hit points equal to 1d6 + 1 per caster level (maximum +10).
