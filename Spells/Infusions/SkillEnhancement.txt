:: Skill Enhancement ::
Transmutation
Level: Armorer 1
Components: V, S, F
Target: Construct or item
Duration: 10 min./level
Saving Throw: Will negates (harmless)
Spell Resistance: Yes (harmless)

This infusion grants a +2 circumstance bonus on a specific skill check (determined by the caster). This bonus increases by 1 for every two caster levels).
