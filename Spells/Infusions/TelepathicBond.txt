:: Telepathic Bond ::
Divination
Level: Armorer 5
Components: V, S, F
Target: Up to one item or construct per three levels within 30 ft.
Saving Throw: Harmless (object)
Spell Resistance: No

As the spell of the same name. You are not automatically included in the effect. Only a willing item bearer or construct is subject to the bond, though different creatures may enter and leave the telepathic effect by taking possession of the appropriate item.
