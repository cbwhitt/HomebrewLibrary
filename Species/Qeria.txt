:: Qeria ::

	Qeria are an ancient subrace of humans that traditionally make their homes in deserts and wastelands. They tend to be nomadic and have a penchant and capacity for mysticism and spiritualism instead of skillful labor, but they are nevertheless as resourceful as all humans are.

	+2 Wisdom, -2 Intelligence
	A qeri's starting Intelligence score is always at least 3. If this adjustment would lower the character's score to 1 or 2, his score is nevertheless 3.
	Humanoid (human)
	Medium: As Medium creatures qeria have no special bonuses or penalties due to their size.
	Qeri base land speed is 30 ft.
	1 extra feat at 1st level
	+2 racial bonus on Survival checks
	Weapon Familiarity: Qeria may treat sand blades and sand whips as martial weapons rather than exotic weapons.
	Automatic Languages: Common and Qerian
	Bonus Languages: Any (other than secret languages, such as Druidic). See the Speak Language skill.
	Favored Class: Mystic
	Level Adjustment: +0
