:: Lumans ::

	+2 Intelligence, -2 Constitution
	Outsider (native, light, primordial)
	Medium: As Medium creatures, lumans have no special bonuses or penalties due to their size.
	Luman base land speed is 30 feet.
	Darkvision
	Radiant: A luman can make their body glow from within at will, shedding light as a torch. They may suppress or activate this glow as a swift action.
	Knowledgeable: Luman characters treat all Knowledge skills as class skills regardless of which class they are.
	+1 bonus on all saving throws
	+1 species bonus on attack rolls against noctans
	+2 species bonus on Search and Spot checks
	Automatic Languages: Common and Luman.
	Bonus Languages: Draconic, Celestial, Gnome, Sylvan, Giant, Elven, Halfling, Aquan, Auran, Terran, Ignan.
	Favored Class: Elementalist
	Level Adjustment: +0
