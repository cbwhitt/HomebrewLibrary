:: Lizardfolk ::

	See regular, LA +1 lizardfolk or 5e materials for description.

	+2 Constitution, -2 Intelligence
	Monstrous Humanoid (reptilian)
	A lizardfolk's starting Intelligence score is always at least 3. If this adjustment would lower a character's score to 1 or 2, their score is nevertheless 3.
	Medium: As Medium creatures, lizardoflk have no special bonuses or penalties due to their size.
	Lizardfolk base land speed is 30 ft.
	+2 species bonus on Survival checks
	+2 species bonus on Heal checks
	+2 species bonus on saves against poison
	Scaly Skin: Lizardfolk receive a +1 species bonus to natural armor.
	Low-Light Vision
	A lizardfolk has a natural bite attack that deals 1d6 damage.
	Automatic Languages: Common and Draconic
	Bonus Languages: Aquan, Noctan, Undercommon, Orc, Gnoll, Golbin
	Favored Class: Barbarian
	Level Adjustment: +0
