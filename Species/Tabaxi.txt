:: Tabaxi ::

	See 5e materials.

	+4 Dexterity, -2 Strength, -2 Constitution, -2 Wisdom
	Monstrous Humanoid (tabaxi)
	Tabaxi base land speed is 40 ft., and tabaxi have a climb speed of 20 ft.
	-4 species penalty to Swim checks
	+2 species bonus on Spot and Listen checks
	+2 species bonus on Hide and Move Silently checks
	Tabaxi have two natural claw attacks that deal 1d4 damage.
	Darkvision
	Automatic Languages: Common and Tabaxi
	Bonus Languages: Halfling, Dwarf, Gnome, Elven, Terran, Auran
	Favored Class: Ranger
