:: Magma Paraelemental ::

Magma paraelementals of all sizes share the following traits:
-----
Type: Elemental (Earth, Fire, Extraplanar)
Speed: 20 ft. (4 squares)
Special Attacks: Magma mastery, heat
Special Qualities: Magma glide, elemental traits
Environment: Paraelemental Plane of Magma
Organization: Solitary, pair, essence (2-3 plus 3-6 smaller elementals)
Treasure: None
Alignment: Usually neutral
Advancement: 1 HD (tiny), 2-3 HD (small), 4-7 HD (medium), 8-15 HD (large), 16-20 HD (huge), 21-23 HD (greater), 24+ HD (elder)
Level Adjustment: --

Magma Mastery (Ex):
	A magma paraelemental receives a +1 bonus on attack and damage rolls against enemies who have suffered fire damage in the past 5 rounds.

Heat (Ex):
	Heat from a magma paraelemental's body causes it to deal additional fire damage on its attacks. Additionally, creatures striking the paraelemental unarmed or with natural weapons suffer this damage.
-----
