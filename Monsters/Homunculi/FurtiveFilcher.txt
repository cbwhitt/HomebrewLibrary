Construction:
	A furtive filcher is assembled from clay, ashes, a pint of the creator's blood, and raw shadowstuff from the Plane of Shadow. The materials cost 50 gp. Creating the body requires a DC 12 Craft (sculpting) or a DC 12 Craft (pottery) check.
	A furtive filcher with more than 1 Hit Die can be created, but each additional Hit Die adds 2000 gp to the COST (not price) to create.
